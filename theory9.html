Theory

The shear strength of soil is the resistance to deformation by continuous shear
displacement of soil particles or upon the action of a shear stress.The purpose of
the direct shear test of soil is to obtain its ultimate shear resistance, its angle
of internal friction, its cohesion, and its shear stress deformation characteristics.
This information is necessary for substructure analysis.
