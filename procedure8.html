Procedure

1. Prepare the specimen for test and measure its parameters.

2. Place the specimen on the base plate of the load frame (sandwiched between the end plates).
>

3. Fix a dial gauge to measure the vertical compression of the specimen.

4. Adjust the gear position on the load frame to give suitable vertical displacement.

5. Start applying the load and record the readings of the proving ring dial and compression dial for every 5 mm compression.

6. Continue loading till failure is complete.
