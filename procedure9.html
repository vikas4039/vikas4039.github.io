Procedure

1. Place the soil in smooth layers in shear box and If a dense tamp the soil.

2. Make the surface of the soil plane. Put the upper grating on stone and loading block on top of soil.


3. Apply the desired normal load. Put the shear box in the loading frame.


4. Before proceeding to test check all adjustments to see that there is no connection between two parts except sand/soil.


5. Start the motor. Take the reading of the shear force and record the reading.


6. Change the value of load and repeat above steps.
