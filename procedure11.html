Falling Head Permeability Test

Home Apparatus Theory Observation Result


Procedure

1. Place the mould with sample in the permeameter, with drainage base and cap having discs that are properly saturated.
2. For the constant head arrangement, the specimen shall be connected through the top inlet to the constant head reservoir.
3. Open the bottom outlet.

4. Establish steady flow of water.

5. The quantity of flow for a convenient time interval may be collected.
